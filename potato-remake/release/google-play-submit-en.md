### Full Description

```text
Growing Potato is a casual raising game where you care for a tiny potato and discover various forms and endings.

Choose daily plans like eating, drinking water, resting, exercising, and photosynthesizing, and watch your potato's status change. Depending on your choices, the potato's personality and growth path will differ, allowing you to unlock and collect multiple unique endings.

Key Features:
- Raise your potato with simple touch controls
- Responsive potato animations based on status changes and growth stages
- 33 unique endings and collection elements to unlock
- Offline support: Play anywhere without internet connection
- Light play: No accounts, no ads, and no in-app purchases

What kind of potato will you grow today?
Take your time and raise your own cute potato.
```
