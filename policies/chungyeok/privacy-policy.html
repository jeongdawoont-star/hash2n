This Privacy Policy describes how the Android app ChungYeok handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not require account sign-in and does not intentionally collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

2. Device Permissions

The app only uses permissions necessary for core gameplay and app operation. No permission is used to collect personal data for advertising profiles.

3. Analytics and Third-Party Services

If analytics or external SDKs are added in the future, this policy will be updated to disclose what data is processed and for what purpose.

4. Data Storage

Gameplay preferences or temporary settings may be stored locally on the device. These local data are not shared with us unless a future feature explicitly states otherwise.

5. Children

The app content is intended for general audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top.
