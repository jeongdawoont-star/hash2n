한국어 버전

This Privacy Policy describes how the Android app Screen Caddie (스크린캐디) handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not require account registration and does not intentionally collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or precise location data. Club distance data and app settings entered by the user are stored only on the device.

2. Device Permissions

This app uses only the INTERNET permission, which is used solely for hotfix data synchronization. Sensitive permissions such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location are not used.

3. Analytics and Third-Party Services

This app uses third-party advertising SDKs (such as Google AdMob) to provide services and display advertisements. In this process, minimal non-personally identifiable information, such as advertising identifiers (ADID) for personalized ad targeting and analytics, may be collected and processed by the third-party service provider.

4. Data Storage

Club distances, swing records, and app settings (language, units, etc.) are stored locally on the device only. No data is transmitted to a server or shared with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for general adult users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If you have concerns about a child's use of this app, please contact us at the address below.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Significant changes will be communicated via the updated date at the top of this document.
